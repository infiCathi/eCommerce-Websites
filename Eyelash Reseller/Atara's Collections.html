Whether you' re an expert at makeup, or only just beginning, we want to help you achieve the goals you have put in front of you. Be confident, be beautiful, be the queen you have always known you are, and let it be displayed on the outside. Pick from our crown collection.

We have Three different styles, just for you!

Flatter

Our "smallest" style of eyelashes. Meant to blend in and subtly flatter your natural features. We wanted to make sure that you could choose to wear eyelashes, even if you had less eyelid space, or just wanted to go for the natural look. For those who do not always want intense lashes.

Enhance

Choose the road in the middle with our "medium" sized eyelashes, meant to enhance the image you want to portray.  These, like the Flatter, are an alternative if you want a little more out of your eyelashes, but have less eyelid space. 

Drama

Dress to impress, and without a doubt, draw attention to your eyes with our Drama lashes, the "large" of the trio. Whether you want to go have a fun night out, or getting ready for some Instagram selfies, these lashes will definitely catch everyone's attention.
